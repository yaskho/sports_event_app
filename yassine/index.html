Core Features Designed for Players

We built the platform to solve the real problems of amateur team sports: coordination and availability.

Post Your Match in Seconds

Organizers can quickly post a new game—select the sport (Football, Padel, Basketball), pick a location, set the date/time, and specify exactly how many players they are missing. The event goes live instantly.

Find Your Game, Your Way

Tired of endless group chats? Filter the event list by your preferred sport, location, or time slot. Our smart filters connect you to the perfect match nearby that fits your schedule.

Instant Join & Updates

Joining an open spot is one tap away. Get instant notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging when someone joins your event or when a match becomes full, ensuring you're always ready to play.

Our Story: Why We Built This App

As passionate sports players—especially football and padel—we constantly faced the same frustrating problem: organizing a casual game felt like a part-time job. We were lucky if we managed to coordinate enough friends to play once a month. We wanted to play more often, but the time spent chasing RSVPs was killing the spontaneity.

The **Sports Match App** was born from that exact frustration. We realized the solution wasn't just *finding* a place to play, but making it effortless to *find the missing players*. Our project focuses solely on solving that coordination gap, ensuring that every player who wants to play, can play.

This project was developed by Rassil Jdidi and Yassine Khouzami as a capstone Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
